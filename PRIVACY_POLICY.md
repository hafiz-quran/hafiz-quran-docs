# Privacy Policy for Hafiz Quran

**Last Updated: December 12, 2024**

## Introduction

Hafiz Quran ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Quran memorization application.

## Information Collection and Use

### Data We Collect

**Local Data Only:**
- **Progress Data:** Your memorization progress, lesson completions, and review schedules are stored locally on your device using SQLite database
- **User Preferences:** App settings and preferences are stored locally on your device
- **Statistics:** Your memorization statistics (streaks, pages memorized, review history) are stored locally on your device

**No Account Required:**
- Hafiz Quran does not require you to create an account
- We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number
- We do not collect any personally identifiable information

### Data Storage

- All user data is stored locally on your device
- Your data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers
- We do not have access to your progress, statistics, or any other data you generate in the app
- Your data is only accessible to you on your device

## Third-Party Services

### Crash Reporting

We use Sentry for crash reporting to help us improve app stability and fix bugs. Sentry may collect:
- Device information (model, operating system version)
- App version
- Crash logs and error reports
- Anonymous usage data

This data is anonymous and cannot be used to identify you personally. For more information, see [Sentry's Privacy Policy](https://sentry.io/privacy/).

### Audio Content

- Audio recitations are bundled with the app or downloaded from our servers
- Audio downloads do not require authentication
- We do not track which audio files you listen to

## Data Security

- All data is stored locally on your device using iOS's secure storage mechanisms
- We do not transmit your personal data over the internet
- Your device's built-in security features protect your data

## Children's Privacy

Hafiz Quran is suitable for users of all ages, including children. Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no special treatment for children's data. Parents can safely allow their children to use the app.

## Data Retention and Deletion

- Your data remains on your device until you delete the app
- Deleting the app removes all local data permanently
- We do not retain backups of your data on our servers
- If you want to delete your data, simply delete the app from your device

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data (it's stored locally on your device)
- Delete your data (by deleting the app)
- Export your data (contact us for assistance if needed)

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
- Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
- Notifying users through the app (for significant changes)

## International Users

Hafiz Quran is available worldwide. Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- No data crosses international borders
- No data is subject to international data transfer regulations
- Your data remains under your control on your device

## Analytics and Tracking

- We do not use analytics services to track your usage
- We do not use advertising or tracking technologies
- We do not share data with third-party advertisers
- The app functions entirely offline after initial setup

## Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

### iOS Permissions

- **Audio Playback:** To play Quranic recitations
- **Local Network (Optional):** For future features like syncing across devices (not currently implemented)
- **Notifications (Optional):** To remind you of daily review sessions

We only request permissions that are necessary for app functionality. You can deny these permissions, but some features may not work properly.
