MEMORIZE THE QURAN WITH CONFIDENCE

Hafiz Quran combines Islamic tradition with modern learning science to make Quran memorization achievable for everyone.

INTELLIGENT CHUNKING
Break ayahs into 2-3 word chunks. Memorize individually, then combine recursively. This follows natural language patterns and reduces cognitive load.

SPACED REPETITION
Review at scientifically optimal intervals. The system groups pages progressively as you advance, keeping daily review manageable even with hundreds of pages memorized.

DAILY HABIT BUILDING
Streaks, progress tracking, and clear goals help you build the consistency that transforms aspiration into achievement.
